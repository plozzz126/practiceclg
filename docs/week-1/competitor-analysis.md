# DevLink Competitor Snapshot

## What we looked at

- Trello for task organization
- Discord for realtime collaboration
- GitHub for project credibility and engineering workflow
- LinkedIn for profile-driven discovery
- Kaggle and hackathon communities for skill-based team formation

## Main takeaways

- Trello is strong at tasks, weak at teammate discovery.
- Discord is strong at chat, weak at structured project filtering.
- LinkedIn is strong at profiles, weak at student project matching.
- Hackathon communities are strong at urgency and team formation, but often weak at profile quality and continuity.

## What DevLink keeps

- Searchable project cards
- Structured skill profiles
- Team join requests with context
- Chat inside the project surface
- Deadline-aware project browsing
